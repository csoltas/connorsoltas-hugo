argument or story?

It's nice to think anyone who hears your startup pitch will remember all the details. Sadly the opposite is true: a sophisticated investor might remember two or three points, and even then, only if you can weave a compelling story through them. Arguments only sound impressive when you're giving them -- afterward, nobody follows up with you.
